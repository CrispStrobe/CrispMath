CrispMath — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

Summary

CrispMath runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data processing

All calculations, history entries, user-defined functions, and notepad content stay on your device in local storage. Nothing is sent to any server.

Optional features that use the network

  • CrispAssist (AI helper) — If you choose to enable it, your query is sent to the AI provider you selected (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic). Your API key is stored locally and sent only to that provider. We never see or relay it.
  • OCR model download — When you download an optional OCR model, the app fetches files from Hugging Face. No personal data is sent; only the model file is downloaded.
  • Crash reports — If the app crashes, a report may be prepared locally. It is only shared if you explicitly choose to file a GitHub issue — no automatic transmission occurs.

Analytics & tracking

CrispMath does not include any analytics SDK, advertising framework, or tracking pixel. We do not use cookies or device fingerprinting.

Third-party services

The app does not embed any third-party SDK that collects data on our behalf. When you use optional network features (CrispAssist, model downloads), those requests go directly to the third-party provider under their own privacy policy.

Children's privacy

CrispMath does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
